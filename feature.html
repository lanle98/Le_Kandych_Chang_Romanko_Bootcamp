Introduction

We created a new online presence for Couples Resort, a Five Star Luxury Resort near Algonquin Park that helps couples stay connected. Owned by the Sorensen Family since 1967, we were tasked with recreating the site with as much of the original branding and content as possible.

Visit

Challenges and Solutions

We created a custom responsive webpage for a simpler user experience, while staying consistent to brand vision. We compiled years of content - including personal blog posts from the owners - into different sections on the site and focused in on the site's primary goal: prompting users to book a room. Users can book online, explore the multiple itineraries, and view photo galleries with ease.

As one of the most popular and reputable resorts among the local community to date, we are proud to have been a part of its growth!
